Breaking: Emergency at GAStech Headquarters Building [Updates]
20 January 2014

19:35 PM: Update, Abila police spokesman Adrien Carman said, "Several GasTech employees appear to be missing. Anyone seeing unusual activity is asked to contact the Abila police."  In response to question about whether a kidnapping is suspected, Carman replied, "The case is under investigation and more information will be released as facts are learned. Please report any unusual activity to the Abila police."
